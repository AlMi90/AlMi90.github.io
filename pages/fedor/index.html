Свистильник
Федор Михайлович

Svistsilnik Fedor Michailovich was born in 1966 in Ukrain – The

He studied in Odessa in ex royal painting school, where studied to paint Vrubel, Brodskiy and others famous painters.

Destiny has abandoned him in Belarus, the motherland of Yanka Kupala and Mark Shagal.

Here he has imbued into landscapes, he taught to paint people 25 years. Paint on a plain, because all that around him very impressive he. He has visited many international plains, his paintings standing in collections all over the world.

He speak:
«I am all know, I am all hear, I am all see in this beautiful world, because I have a river, forest and stones. These things tell me about all that happens in this world.
